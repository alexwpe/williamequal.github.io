Composer & Sound Designer for Games and Media


Music and sound effects can tranform your game or media into a fully immersive experience that addresses all the human senses. I design my music in a way that will catch your audience emotionally and draws them into the experience you created.

My name's Alex "Wiliam E." Gleich and I'm a composer, songwriter and sound designer from Germany. My work is deeply rooted in a time, when melodies had to be simple but memorable. Yet I create music and effects that sound modern but familiar, combining the art of variation of melodies with modern sounding instruments and pristine audio quality.

What I can do for you

Music is my passion and I can't remember a time where I didn't play an instrument or recorded music of any kind. I love creating audio-related things and hope to propel your game or media forward with engagement and reliability with several years of industry-experience in the field of interactive media and games.

Music Composition:
There is only two pricing tiers to keep things simple: 'Abstract' (for retro, ambient and electronic music) and 'Realistic' (orchestral and band music). Even better, we'll settle on a price per song, not per minute with indie-friendly rates.

Sound Design:
Musical and/or abstract sound effects and implementation, basic Foley SFX

Game Trailers:
I can produce trailers for your game! In case you already made one, I could help out with scoring the trailer as well.

Voice Acting:
Voice acting, especially in German or with very slight german accent in English is available, too.

Help & Education:
Need help with audio in your game? Feel free to reach out to me anytime! Have a look at my YouTube videos too: host of the YouTube show "Game Audio Lookout"
